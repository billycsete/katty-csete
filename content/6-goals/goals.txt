Goals Heading: Goals

----

Goals List: 

- Challenge myself to learn new skills and maintain variety in what I practice through continuing education, skill set certifications, and patient caseload in order to continue developing as a physical therapist
- Continue to grow and develop as a therapist throughout my entire career
- Keep up with current events and research involving physical therapy by subscribing to journals and reading articles consistently
- Advocate for my profession by getting involved in local and national organizations
- Provide support and care for each of my patients by being an ally on their healthcare team and individualize their plan of care specific towards their recovery
